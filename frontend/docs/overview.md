# Overview of Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Welcome to the AI-Native Textbook

This textbook provides a comprehensive exploration of **Physical AI** and **Humanoid Robotics**, combining traditional educational content with cutting-edge AI-powered learning experiences.

### What You'll Learn

- **Physical AI Fundamentals**: Understanding the integration of perception, reasoning, and action in physical environments
- **Humanoid Robotics**: Design, control, and applications of human-like robots
- **Vision-Language-Action Systems**: Modern approaches to embodied intelligence
- **ROS 2 Integration**: Practical implementation of robotic systems
- **AI-Powered Learning**: Using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) for personalized education

### How to Use This Textbook

This textbook features an **AI assistant** that can answer questions about the full book content or specific text selections. Look for the AI Assistant links throughout the content to access this feature.

### Target Audience

- Advanced students in robotics and AI
- Robotics engineers and researchers
- AI practitioners interested in embodied intelligence
- Faculty members evaluating advanced robotics curricula
